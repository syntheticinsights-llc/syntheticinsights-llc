Introducing Smartodo

One task system across every Apple device

Smartodo unifies your tasks, notes, and projects with instant sync between Mac, iPhone, and iPad. Work offline, stay in control of your data, and rely on your personal AI agent when you need an extra hand.

  • Capture ideas in seconds with natural language input.
  • Let the AI agent break plans into actionable steps.
  • Keep everything secure with on-device storage.
