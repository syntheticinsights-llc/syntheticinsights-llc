Why Smartodo

Privacy-first productivity with an AI boost

Local-first, smooth sync all the devices

Smartodo keeps every task on-device and syncs securely through your own WebDAV storage—privacy matters, so your work stays yours.

Native OneDrive and other providers are on the roadmap, and you can already stay fully in control by hosting storage you trust.

Dependency graph

Visualize relationships, uncover blockers, and keep complex initiatives moving with a live dependency graph that stays in sync with your boards.

Zoom in on priority chains or step back to see the whole network so critical work never slips.

AI agent

Collaborate with a secure cloud AI agent that drafts plans, recommends next steps, and keeps context aligned with your goals.

You decide what leaves your workspace—we redact sensitive details, encrypt requests, and never store your prompts or responses beyond each session.

Filters

Slice work by status, title keywords, owners, and dependency relationships to spotlight exactly what matters right now.

Save reusable filters for reviews, roadmap checkpoints, or dependency sweeps and keep every teammate aligned in seconds.
