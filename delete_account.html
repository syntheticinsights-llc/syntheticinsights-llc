Account removal

Delete account

This is the official Smartodo account deletion page provided by Synthetic Insights LLC.

Important

This action is permanent. Sign in with your Smartodo account email and password, then type confirm delete to proceed.

How it works
1

Sign in with your Smartodo account email and password.

2

Type confirm delete and submit the request.

3

Your Smartodo account and account-related data will be permanently deleted.

What gets deleted
  • Account-related Smartodo data associated with your account will be deleted.
  • Active subscriptions must be managed separately in the App Store or Google Play.
  • Task data and files stored locally on your devices or in your own cloud storage remain under your control and are not automatically removed by deleting your account.
